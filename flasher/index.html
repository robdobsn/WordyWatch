WordyWatch

Flash firmware to your watch via USB

Your browser does not support WebSerial.
Please use Google Chrome or Microsoft Edge. WebSerial is not allowed in this context.
Make sure you are accessing this page over HTTPS.

How to flash

  1. Connect your WordyWatch to your computer via USB-C
  2. Press and hold the power button on the watch
  3. Click Install WordyWatch Firmware above
  4. Select the serial port for your watch
  5. Keep holding the power button until flashing begins, then release
  6. Wait for the flash to complete
Requires Chrome or Edge on desktop. WebSerial is not supported in Firefox, Safari, or mobile browsers.
